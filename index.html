Summary

British Airways PTFS was founded with the goal of bringing the excitement, professionalism, and realism of global aviation to life within Pilot Training Flight Simulator (PTFS). By recreating the operations of the real-world British Airways, we offer our members an authentic airline experience from pre-flight planning to landing at international destinations.

Operating out of our hubs at London Heathrow, London Gatwick and London City (BA CityFlyer), British Airways provides a fully immersive environment where pilots can manage everything from short domestic hops to long-haul intercontinental flights.

Our community is designed for aviation enthusiasts of all levels, whether you are a new pilot learning the basics of flight procedures or an experienced member taking command of widebody aircraft. Members can take on various roles, including pilots, staff, and associates, each contributing to the airline's smooth operation.

Pilots participate in scheduled flights and special events, earn ranks and rewards, and gain access to advanced aircraft and routes. Meanwhile, staff members, directors, and the Executive Board ensure operations run efficiently, maintaining realism, safety, and a welcoming community atmosphere.

History

British Airways PTFS was established on 28 May 2024 by Phonetic (Robín), the General Director, with the mission of revitalizing the airline within the PTC community.

After a period of inactivity, the server successfully relaunched operations on 30 September 2025, marking a fresh start for its members. The airline was created by Robín, Giorgio, and Caleb, with Caleb later stepping down. Together, they laid the foundations for a professional and engaging virtual airline experience that mirrors real-world aviation operations.

Since its relaunch, British Airways PTFS has quickly grown to over 100 members, demonstrating the strong interest and engagement within the community. With its dedicated leadership and active member base, the airline is poised to become one of the largest and most respected virtual airlines in the PTC community.

Experience the skies

British Airways PTFS offers a comprehensive and carefully structured range of virtual airline services that are specifically designed to deliver a highly professional, realistic, and deeply immersive flight simulation experience. Pilots are able to participate in well-organized daily scheduled flights, a wide selection of long-haul and regional routes, and a variety of special event operations, all of which strictly follow realistic airline procedures, authentic operational standards, and real-world aviation practices to ensure maximum realism and consistency across every flight.

Classes

British Airways PTFS offers multiple cabin classes to create a truly realistic and immersive airline environment that mirrors real-world British Airways operations. First Class represents the highest level of luxury and roleplay, featuring private suites, premium onboard service, and an exclusive experience designed for the most prestigious flights. Business Class, also known as Club World, provides flat-bed seating, priority procedures, lounge access, and enhanced services tailored for international and long-haul operations. Premium Economy, branded as World Traveller Plus, offers a balanced combination of comfort and efficiency, with additional legroom, upgraded amenities, and improved service for mid- to long-haul journeys. Economy Class, known as World Traveller, accurately simulates standard airline operations with realistic seating configurations and service procedures, making it ideal for regional routes and short-haul flights.

Crew

New members benefit from dedicated and structured training programs that are designed to support both beginners and developing pilots. These programs cover essential aviation skills such as navigation, communication procedures, flight planning, and emergency protocols to ensure safe and confident operations. At the same time, experienced crew members have the opportunity to progress through advanced ranks, complete missions, and take part in special operations, allowing them to earn recognition, unlock rewards, and demonstrate their expertise within the airline.

Community Engagement

British Airways PTFS is built around a vibrant and engaging community where members can take part in group flights, seasonal events, special operations, and competitions. The server encourages active collaboration, teamwork, and interaction between all members, staff, and the Executive Board, fostering a strong sense of belonging. Members can contribute ideas, participate in planning events, and help shape the growth of the airline, ensuring that every experience within BA PTFS is both immersive and enjoyable.

Executive Club

Executive Club is a premium program that rewards active members with virtual perks, exclusive privileges, and access to special flights. Higher-tier members gain priority opportunities and recognition, becoming an integral part of the airline's community and operations within PTFS. Check more here.

Professional Staff

With a professional staff team and executive leadership, British Airways PTFS ensures smooth operations, a safe environment, and strict adherence to realistic procedures. Every service is designed to combine realism, skill development, and community interaction, creating one of the most engaging and respected virtual airline experiences within the PTFS platform.
